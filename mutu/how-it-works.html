Simple Booking Journey

How HomeFix works from booking to completion

Book in minutes, get matched with a trusted professional, and track every step until the job is completed.

Step-by-step process

Step 1

Choose your service

Browse HomeFix services and pick what you need, from plumbing and electrical work to appliance setup and repairs.

Step 2

Select date and preferred time

Choose the most convenient slot for your schedule. You can include notes about your issue for faster preparation.

Step 3

Get booking confirmation

We confirm your request through WhatsApp with your booking details, estimated arrival window, and assigned professional.

Step 4

Professional arrives and starts work

A verified HomeFix pro arrives, inspects the task, and proceeds with the service while keeping you updated on progress.

Step 5

Review and complete

After completion, you review the work, receive final details, and close the booking with confidence.

What to prepare before your appointment

  • Keep the work area accessible for the technician.
  • Share clear photos or short notes for complex issues.
  • Be available during the confirmed arrival window.
  • List any material or brand preferences in advance.

What happens after the service

  • You get a completion update and service summary.
  • Our support team is available for follow-up needs.
  • Recurring maintenance can be scheduled quickly.
  • Your feedback helps us keep quality high.
